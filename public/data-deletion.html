Data Deletion

Last updated May 20, 2026

Short version: Brand Gita does not store your Instagram or Facebook data on our servers. Your content stays on your device. To remove your connection, revoke access directly in your Instagram or Facebook settings — or email us and we will handle it within 30 days.

What Data Brand Gita Accesses via Google / Instagram / Facebook

When you connect your accounts to Brand Gita, we request access to:

  • Your account name and profile — used to identify your account inside the app (Instagram and Google)
  • Permission to publish to your Instagram feed — so Brand Gita can post content to Instagram on your behalf, when you choose to publish
  • Permission to upload and publish to your YouTube channel — so Brand Gita can post videos to YouTube on your behalf, when you choose to publish

We store only your OAuth access token locally on your device, inside your operating system's secure credential store. We do not upload, cache, or retain your Instagram credentials or media on any Brand Gita server.

How to Delete Your Data

Because Brand Gita stores credentials locally on your device, deletion is straightforward:

  1. Disconnect your accounts in Brand Gita. Open Brand Gita → Settings → disconnect your Instagram and Google accounts. This deletes the stored tokens from your device's secure credential store.
  2. Uninstall the app (optional). Uninstalling Brand Gita removes all locally stored data, including any cached profile information.

Request Deletion by Email

If you would like us to confirm deletion of any data associated with your account, email us at support@brandgita.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". Include the email address or Instagram username associated with your account. We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

Questions

Contact us at support@brandgita.com. For full details on how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.
