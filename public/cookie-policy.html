2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites function correctly, remember your preferences, and provide information to the site operator.

Similar technologies include:

  • Local storage — data stored in your browser that persists between sessions
  • Session storage — data stored in your browser that is cleared when you close the tab
  • Pixel tags / web beacons — tiny invisible images that can confirm a page was loaded or an email was opened

5. How to Manage Cookies

5.1 Cookie Settings Banner

When you first visit brandgita.com, a cookie settings banner will be displayed. You can accept all cookies, accept only necessary cookies, or manage your preferences individually. You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the website footer.

5.2 Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies currently set
  • Block all cookies or specific types of cookies
  • Delete cookies at any time
  • Be notified before a cookie is set

Browser-specific guidance:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions

Note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect the functionality of the website or your ability to log in.

5.3 Opt Out of Analytics

To opt out of analytics cookies specifically, either:

  • Select "Necessary only" in the cookie settings banner, or
  • Email support@brandgita.com with "Cookie Opt-Out" in the subject line

6. Do Not Track
