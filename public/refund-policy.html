Refund Policy

Effective date: 20 May 2026 · Last updated: 20 May 2026

Current Status — Free Service

Brand Gita is currently provided free of charge. No payment is required to access or use the Services, and this Policy has no current operational effect. It is published so you know what to expect if and when paid plans are introduced.

Monthly Subscriptions

When paid plans are introduced, they will be offered on a month-to-month basis only. You may cancel at any time through your account settings or by contacting support@brandgita.com. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current monthly billing period, and you will not be charged for future months.

No refunds are provided for unused time, partial months, or change of mind.

When Refunds Are Available

Refunds will be issued only in the following circumstances:

  • You were charged in error (for example, double-billed or charged after cancellation)
  • The Services were materially non-functional due to a fault on our side, and the issue was not resolved within 7 days after you reported it to support@brandgita.com
  • Applicable law requires a refund, including rights under the New Zealand Consumer Guarantees Act 1993

Consumer Rights (New Zealand)

If you are a consumer under the Consumer Guarantees Act 1993 (NZ), you have statutory rights that cannot be excluded by this Policy. Where a service fails to meet a consumer guarantee under that Act, your remedies are determined by the Act. This Policy operates alongside and does not limit those statutory rights.

How to Request a Refund

Email support@brandgita.com with your account email, the date and amount of the charge, and the reason for your request. I will acknowledge within 5 business days and aim to resolve within 14 days. Approved refunds are returned to the original payment method (processing typically takes 5–10 business days after approval).

Updates

This Policy may be updated before any paid plans launch. Material changes will be notified in accordance with the Terms of Service.
