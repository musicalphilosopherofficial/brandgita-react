1. What Information Do I Collect?

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

When you register, use, or contact me about the Services, you may provide:

  • Name and email address — used to create and manage your account
  • Username — used to identify your account within the Services
  • OAuth access tokens (Instagram, YouTube) — stored locally on your device only, inside your operating system's secure credential store; used solely to authenticate publishing actions on your behalf
  • Support communications — descriptions of issues, screen recordings, or video excerpts you voluntarily submit when seeking assistance

1.2 Information Collected from Third Parties (Indirect Collection)

When you choose to connect a social media account, I receive limited profile information from that provider:

  • Google / YouTube: Account name, email address, and profile picture; permission to upload and publish to your YouTube channel
  • Instagram: Account name, profile picture, and permission to publish to your Instagram feed
  • Facebook: Basic account information including name and email address, and any other information you choose to make public

I collect only the minimum information necessary for the Services to function. I do not access your contacts, friends lists, private messages, or any data beyond what is described above.

I notify you of this indirect collection at the time you connect your social account and in this Policy, in accordance with New Zealand's Privacy Act 2020 (Information Privacy Principle 3A).

1.3 Diagnostic and Usage Data

I may collect anonymous technical information to improve the reliability and performance of the Services — such as feature usage patterns, error reports, and crash logs. This information does not identify you personally and contains no data received from Meta, Google, or any other connected social platform (no platform user IDs, profile information, access tokens, or other platform-provided data). You may opt out at any time in the app's Settings → Privacy.

1.4 Hardware and Performance Data

With your explicit opt-in consent, I may collect device performance metrics (such as processing speed and resource usage) during app operation. This is entirely opt-in and can be disabled at any time in the app's Settings. Hardware and performance data is never linked to your identity on Meta, Google, or any other connected platform.

1.5 Payment Data

If paid features are introduced in the future, payment data will be collected and processed directly by the payment processor in use at that time. I will not receive, store, or have access to your full payment card details. This Policy will be updated to name the relevant payment processor before any paid features are launched.

1.6 Cookies and Tracking Data

The website uses cookies and similar technologies. See Section 5 for full details.

1.7 What I Do Not Collect

I do not collect or process:

  • Sensitive personal information (racial or ethnic origin, political opinions, religious beliefs, health data, genetic or biometric data, sexual orientation, criminal records)
  • Precise geolocation data
  • Browsing history or behavioural tracking data
  • Financial account details
  • Information from children under 18

2. How Do I Process Your Information?

I process your personal information only for the following purposes:

  • Account creation and authentication — to allow you to register, log in, and manage your account
  • Service delivery — to provide the features you have signed up for, including publishing content to connected social accounts on your instruction
  • Service improvement — to analyse usage patterns, diagnose problems, and improve reliability and functionality
  • Communications — to respond to your enquiries and provide support
  • Marketing — with your consent, to inform you about updates, new features, or related news; you may opt out at any time
  • Security and fraud prevention — to detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity
  • Legal compliance — to comply with applicable laws, regulations, and lawful requests
  • Protection of vital interests — to prevent serious harm in urgent circumstances

I do not sell your personal information to third parties. I do not use your personal information to train any artificial intelligence model.

3. What Legal Bases Do I Rely On?

I process your personal information only where I have a valid legal basis:

  • Consent — where you have given clear consent for a specific purpose (e.g., hardware data collection; marketing emails). You may withdraw consent at any time by contacting me. Withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before the withdrawal.
  • Contractual necessity — where processing is necessary to provide the Services you have signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests — where processing is necessary for legitimate interests such as fraud prevention, product improvement, and user communications. I have weighed these interests against your privacy rights and am satisfied they are proportionate.
  • Legal obligation — where I am required to process your information to comply with applicable law or to establish, exercise, or defend legal claims.
  • Vital interests — where processing is necessary to protect life.

Canada: For Canadian users, I rely on express or implied consent where applicable, or on exceptions permitted by applicable Canadian law for fraud detection, safety, and legal compliance.

Note: The Services are not available to EEA, UK, or Swiss residents. I do not rely on GDPR or UK GDPR as a processing framework.

6. Artificial Intelligence Features

6.1 On-Device AI Processing

Brand Gita includes certain AI models (such as audio enhancement) that run entirely on your device. These models process your media locally — no data is transmitted to any external server. On-device models do not send telemetry, usage data, or user content to Brand Gita or any third party.

6.2 Bring Your Own AI (BYOAI)

Brand Gita's core creative features use AI tools you already have access to on your machine. Currently supported providers are Claude (Anthropic), Google Gemini, and Ollama (local open-source models). OpenAI and ChatGPT support is planned for a future release. When you use these features:

  • For cloud-based providers (Claude, Gemini): your content is transmitted directly from your device to that provider
  • For local models (Ollama): all processing stays on your device — no data is transmitted externally
  • Brand Gita does not intermediate, access, store, log, or process those communications
  • Brand Gita does not train any AI model on your content
  • The AI provider's own terms of service and privacy policy govern how your data is handled

Supported providers: Claude (Anthropic), Google Gemini, Ollama (local). Brand Gita is not responsible for how any third-party AI provider handles your data.

6.3 Your Responsibilities

You are responsible for:

  • Reviewing and complying with the terms of any AI provider you connect
  • Ensuring you have the right to submit any content to your chosen provider
  • Reviewing all AI-generated outputs before publishing — Brand Gita makes no representations about the accuracy, legality, originality, or intellectual property status of any AI-generated content

6.4 Disconnecting an AI Provider

Remove or change your AI provider at any time through the app's Settings screen.

7. Social Media Logins

The Services allow you to register and log in using your Google, Instagram, or Facebook account. When you do, I receive limited profile information from that platform — typically your name, email address, and profile picture — along with an OAuth access token.

OAuth tokens are stored only on your local device in your operating system's secure credential store. I do not upload, cache, or retain your tokens or credentials on any Brand Gita server.

I use information received from social platforms only for the purposes described in this Policy. I do not control how those platforms independently collect or use your information and recommend you review their privacy policies.

To disconnect a social account:

  1. Open Brand Gita → Settings → disconnect your account (deletes the locally stored token immediately)
  2. Revoke Brand Gita's access directly in your Instagram, Google, or Facebook account settings
  3. Email support@brandgita.com — I will confirm deletion within 30 days
