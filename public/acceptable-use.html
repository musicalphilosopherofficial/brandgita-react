1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") sets out the rules for using Brand Gita's services ("Services"). It applies to all users and forms part of the Terms of Service. Capitalised terms used but not defined here have the meanings given in the Terms of Service.

By using the Services, you agree to this AUP. Violations may result in immediate suspension or termination of your account.

Questions? Contact support@brandgita.com.

2. The Short Version

Use Brand Gita to create and publish legitimate branded content. Do not use it to harm others, break the law, violate platform rules, infringe intellectual property, or abuse the service. Be responsible for everything you publish.

3. Prohibited Content

You may not use the Services to create, process, store, transmit, or publish any content that:

3.1 Is Illegal or Harmful

  • Violates any applicable law or regulation in any jurisdiction
  • Promotes, facilitates, or encourages any illegal activity
  • Contains child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or any sexual content involving minors — this is an absolute prohibition with no exceptions
  • Constitutes harassment, bullying, threats, or incitement to violence against any person or group
  • Promotes terrorism, extremism, or mass violence
  • Facilitates fraud, phishing, identity theft, or financial crime
  • Violates any person's privacy rights, including doxxing or publishing private information without consent

3.2 Infringes Third-Party Rights

  • Infringes the copyright, trademark, patent, trade secret, or other intellectual property rights of any person or entity
  • Uses copyrighted music, footage, images, or other media without the necessary licences or permissions
  • Misappropriates another person's likeness, voice, or identity without their consent
  • Uses third-party brand assets, logos, or trademarks in a way that is misleading, unauthorised, or constitutes passing off

3.3 Is Deceptive or Misleading

  • Constitutes false advertising, misleading claims, or deceptive commercial practices under any applicable law
  • Impersonates any person, organisation, or brand in a way that is likely to deceive
  • Contains false or materially misleading statements of fact
  • Uses AI-generated content to create deepfakes or synthetic media that misrepresents a real person's actions, words, or likeness without their consent

3.4 Violates Platform Terms

  • Violates the terms of service, community guidelines, or advertising policies of Instagram, YouTube, Facebook, or any other third-party platform you publish to via the Services
  • Constitutes spam or unsolicited mass messaging on any platform
  • Artificially inflates engagement metrics (views, likes, followers) through inauthentic means

3.5 Is Otherwise Harmful

  • Contains malware, viruses, or malicious code
  • Is designed to damage, disable, or impair any system or service
  • Exploits or abuses the Services in ways not intended or authorised

In addition to the content restrictions above, you may not:

  • Use the Services for any commercial purpose that involves reselling, sublicensing, or white-labelling access to the Services without my prior written consent

  • Circumvent, disable, or interfere with security features, access controls, or geographic restrictions
  • Scrape, crawl, or use automated tools to extract data from the Services
  • Submit false or misleading information when registering for or using the Services
  • Create accounts for the purpose of circumventing a prior suspension or ban
  • Use the Services in a way that imposes an unreasonable or disproportionate load on the infrastructure

When using Brand Gita's AI features — whether on-device models or via a connected third-party AI provider — you may not:

  • Submit content to any AI provider that you do not have the right to submit
